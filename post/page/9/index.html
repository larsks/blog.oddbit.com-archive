  5. nova-docker and environment variables

    Published Thu, Aug 28, 2014

    I’ve been playing with Docker a bit recently, and decided to take a look at the nova-docker driver for OpenStack. The nova-docker driver lets Nova, the OpenStack Compute service, spawn Docker containers instead of hypervisor-based servers. For certain workloads, this leads to better resource utilization than you would get with a hypervisor-based solution, while at the same time givin you better support for multi-tenancy and flexible networking than you get with Docker by itself.
